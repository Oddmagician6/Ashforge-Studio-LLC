How It Works

The Commission Process

From first inquiry to final delivery - here's what working with Ashforge Studio looks like.

01

Inquiry

Fill out the commission form with your project brief. I'll respond within 2 business days to confirm scope, timeline, and fit.

02

Brief & Agreement

We align on deliverables, word count, revision rounds, and deadline. A simple agreement is signed and a deposit secures your spot.

03

Draft

I write. You'll receive a full first draft by the agreed date - structured, playtested where applicable, and ready for your feedback.

04

Revisions

Up to two rounds of revisions are included. I work with your notes to sharpen tone, balance mechanics, and nail the details.

05

Delivery

Final files delivered in your preferred format (Google Doc, Word, Markdown, or InDesign-ready). Balance of payment due on delivery.
