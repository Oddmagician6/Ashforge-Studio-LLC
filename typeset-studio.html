What It Does

A whole typesetting studio, offline

Typeset Studio does the fiddly, expensive part of self-publishing, laying out a professional book interior the way a designer would, but from a simple browser window on your own PC.

Real book typesetting

Drop caps, running heads, page numbers, mirrored margins, scene breaks, a proper table of contents, real hyphenation and smart quotes. The details a printed book actually needs.

Genre styles, reusable

Nine ready-made interior styles: literary, thriller, romance, fantasy, sci-fi, mass-market and more. Build a style once, then duplicate it per book and tweak only what changes.

Cover Studio

Pick a genre house style, fill in your title, and get a professional designed cover, with backgrounds, emblems, and a full front + spine + back print wrap sized to your page count.

Write in the app

A built-in manuscript editor with live styling, a chapter outline, find & replace, word count, and autosave. Or just import the .docx, .md, or .txt you already have.

PDF & EPUB export

Export a print-ready PDF with subsetted, embedded fonts and an EPUB 3 e-book from the same manuscript, plus a preflight report and spine / margin calculator for KDP and IngramSpark.

Private by design

Everything runs locally in your browser against an app on your own PC. No cloud upload, no account, no watermark, no per-book fee. Your unpublished manuscript stays yours.
